TAG
[tæg]
noun
  1. a label attached to someone or something for identification. "he gave his pet a collar with a metal name tag" synonyms: label, ticket, badge, mark, tab, sticker, flag, stamp
  2. a small piece or part attached to a main body.
verb
  1. attach a label to. "the bottles were tagged with colour-coded labels" synonyms: label, mark, ticket, identify, flag
  2. add to something as an afterthought. "she meant to tag her question on at the end" synonyms: add, tack, join, attach, append, affix
